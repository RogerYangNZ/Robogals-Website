Become a Sponsor

Support Robogals by becoming a sponsor and help us inspire young girls to pursue a career in STEM. Your contributions make a big impact on our ability to reach more students and offer more workshops. Simply use the contact form or email auckland.partnerships@robogals.org

For Schools and Libraries

If you're a school or library and want Robogals to host a free STEM workshop for your students, we're here to help. Our workshops are designed to inspire and engage young minds in the world of science and technology. You can do this by email us at: auckland.schools@robogals.org

Join Robogals as a volunteer and help us deliver exciting STEM workshops. Gain valuable teaching experience, contribute to your community, and help us empower the next generation of engineers and scientists. Sign up to our newsletter and join our discord server.
