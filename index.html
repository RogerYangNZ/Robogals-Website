Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, engage, and empower young women into engineering and related fields of study to develop an equitable STEM pipeline from schools to universities and workplaces.

Who Are We

Robogals Auckland is a student-run non-profit organization responsible for empowering the next generation of engineers. We show young women the opportunities available to them in STEM fields and strive to close the gender gap to achieve gender equity.

What We Do

Inspire, engage, and empower young women into STEM. Robogals Auckland supports the next generation of future engineers in schools, universities, and industries.
